The sweet century

Our story

Our story begins over 50 years ago, in the days when Grandpa Haim Spitzer immigrated to Israel. He immediately recognized the need for a basic product - bread. Hish Meher rolled up his sleeves and opened a small, home bakery. Not much time passed and he added special and designed whipped creams to the range of products, along with cakes and cookies, mainly for banquet halls and parties. One of Grandfather Chaim's joys was to make a bride and groom happy with challah and special cakes for their special day. He distributed all the products he produced himself while riding his bicycle, and later on a horse and cart. Those were magical and sweet years.
